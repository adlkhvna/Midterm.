About us

Gourmet dining in Sushiland

Sushiland sushi bar, exquisitely located in Nur-Sultan city, in Turkestan 34 district, has opened its doors to those looking for Japanese cuisine. They provide a wide variety of culinary delights including snacks, sushi, sashimi, freestyle rolls, teriyaki, noodles, donburi and desserts. We pride ourselves on the fact that all of their sauces such as teriyaki and ponzu sauce are made locally. They have a variety of signature rolls that have inspired a new generation of Japanese craftsmen. With over 140 different dishes, you'll find plenty of options to suit your favorite food - be it seafood, beef, poultry or vegetarian options.

Our Story

Gourmet dining in Sushiland

Sushi bar Sushiland, owned by a students of Astana IT University, whose dream was to open their own Japanese restaurant, is their own Cinderella story. They were fascinated by the Japanese pursuit of excellence in food and artistic expression. Although they are all good chefs themselves, they are fortunate to have a chef specially trained by a Japanese sushi master in Korea with over ten years of experience. So if you want to know what it is like to have a fairy godmother, come to Sushiland, where your culinary dream can come true.
